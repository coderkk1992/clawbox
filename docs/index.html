The Problem with AI Assistants

Powerful tools shouldn't require dangerous tradeoffs.

⚠️

Full System Access

Most AI assistants run directly on your machine with access to all your files, credentials, and data. One wrong command and things go sideways.

💻

CLI Only

Powerful AI tools often require terminal knowledge. Not everyone is comfortable with command lines, and that shouldn't be a barrier.

✨

ClawBox Solves Both

A beautiful native app that runs your AI in a sandboxed VM. Your assistant gets tools. Your system stays protected.
